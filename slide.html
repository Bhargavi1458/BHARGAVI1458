The main theme of this project is provide awareness about the profiles of IT companies present in our country. It will give all the details of firms like company established year, employability , CEO's in present and previous, location of company, and recent project's etc., This project is aimed to provide information to users friendly manner. And also in this application includeing like previous written exam question papers, and some links(i., if any user need to upload some information, or additional information about company). By this stakeholders can update what are the day-to-day changers and they can privide the new company details.
There are many companies are providing their profiles individually on their one website. And there is huge data, it is difficult to analysis exact data in peak stage before attending the interview. We are going to proposed all companies informations in one website and that will be help to students or user's how to know about company details exactly. In this app infrastructure like it has company logo, description about companies in our country. The intention of this appilcation is to make user to face an interview with full company profile.
