History

The Government of India constituted the International Airports Authority of India (AAI) in 1972 to mange the natio's international airports while the natonal Airport Authority(NAA) was Constituted in 1986 to look after domestic airports. The organizations were merged in April 1995 by an Act of Parliament and was named as Airports Authority of India(AAI). This new oranization was to be responsible for creating upgrading, maintainng and managing civil aviation infrastructure both on the group and air space in the country.
